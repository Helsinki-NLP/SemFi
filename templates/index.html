SemFi provides you with an online access to word relations obtained from The Finnish Internet Parsebank. These relations have successfully been used in the task of computational creativity in Poem Machine to create Finnish poetry automatically.

You can also download these relations to your own computer. This data is released under CC BY-SA 4.0. © 2015-2017 Mika Hämäläinen
